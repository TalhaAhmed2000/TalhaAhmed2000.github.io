Bio
---

I am a Research Assistant at the [Algorithms in Theory and Practice (ATP) Lab](https://web.lums.edu.pk/~hmd/teaching.html), [Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS)](https://lums.edu.pk/), working under the supervision of [Dr. Hassan Mohy-ud-Din](https://web.lums.edu.pk/~hmd/). I graduated with **High Distinction** in a joint B.S. in Mathematics–Economics with a minor in Computer Science from LUMS in 2024. My research focuses on designing **interpretable, robust, and resource-efficient generative models** for practical deployment in biomedical and computational imaging settings.
